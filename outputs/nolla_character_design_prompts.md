# Nolla Instagram キャラクターデザイン プロンプト集

Nano Banana 2（Gemini）で使用。各プロンプトをそのままコピペしてください。

---

## アカウントA：人間キャラ（3パターン）

### A-1：ゆるポップ線画スタイル（まめきちまめこ寄り）
```
Character design sheet for a 4-panel manga series. Simple loose line art style, thick outlines, minimal details, flat pastel colors, white background.

Two characters side by side with multiple expressions (happy, surprised, tired, laughing):

1. Parent character: A young mother with messy short hair and round glasses. Oversized hoodie. Exaggerated round face, dot eyes, simple mouth. Always looks slightly tired but warm.

2. Child character: A small boy (age 5-6) with spiky uneven hair. Big round head, tiny body. Wears a striped t-shirt that's too big for him. Has an intense focused stare and a big innocent smile.

Style: Japanese comedy manga, deformed proportions, kawaii, hand-drawn feel. Like Atashinchi or Mamekichi Mameko manga style. No realistic proportions.
```
**特徴**: シンプル線画、ドット目、パステルカラー。まめきちまめこに近いポップさ。

---

### A-2：丸っこいデフォルメスタイル（あたしんち寄り）
```
Character design sheet for a 4-panel manga series. Rounded deformed cartoon style, bold black outlines, soft warm colors, white background.

Two characters side by side with multiple expressions (happy, confused, exhausted, proud):

1. Parent character: A mother with a round potato-shaped head, small eyes, big cheeks. Hair in a simple ponytail. Wears an apron over casual clothes. Short and chubby proportions, 3-head-tall deformed body.

2. Child character: A small boy with a perfectly round head, huge sparkling eyes (contrast with mom's small eyes). Wears a yellow hat and overalls. Always slightly tilting his head. 2-head-tall super deformed.

Style: Japanese slice-of-life comedy manga, like Atashinchi. Warm, nostalgic, humorous. Exaggerated expressions for comedy.
```
**特徴**: じゃがいも頭の母＋まんまる大きい目の子。親子のギャップがコメディになる。

---

### A-3：おしゃれゆるイラストスタイル（インスタ映え寄り）
```
Character design sheet for a 4-panel manga series. Modern cute illustration style, thin clean lines, muted earth tone colors with one accent color (mustard yellow), white background.

Two characters side by side with multiple expressions (smile, pout, sleepy, excited):

1. Parent character: A gender-neutral parent with fluffy bob hair, closed relaxed eyes (like >_<), small nose, rosy cheeks. Wears a simple knit sweater. Soft rounded silhouette, 4-head-tall proportions.

2. Child character: A toddler (age 4-5) with a big round head and tiny limbs. One tuft of hair sticking up. Always barefoot. Huge curious eyes. Wears a simple one-piece outfit. Holds a favorite toy (small dinosaur) in every scene.

Style: Modern Japanese Instagram illustration, Scandinavian-influenced kawaii, clean and minimal. Warm and cozy atmosphere.
```
**特徴**: アースカラー＋マスタードイエロー。おしゃれで落ち着いた雰囲気。インスタの雰囲気に合う。

## アカウントB：オリジナルキャラ（3パターン）

### B-1：柴犬ベース超デフォルメスタイル
```
Character design sheet for a 4-panel manga series. Soft rounded style, thick outlines, warm pastel colors (cream, light orange, soft brown), white background.

Two characters side by side with multiple expressions (happy, startled, sleepy, proud):

1. Parent character: An extremely deformed round creature. Inspired loosely by shiba inu but SO heavily stylized that it is NOT recognizable as a dog. Just a round warm-colored blob with tiny triangle ears, dot eyes, and a small round nose. No snout, no tail visible, no paws - just stubby mitten hands. Cream and soft orange coloring. Stands upright on two tiny legs. Gentle calm expression by default. Medium size.

2. Child character: Almost identical shape and design as parent but 40% smaller. Same round blob body, same tiny triangle ears, same dot eyes. ONE difference only: has a small star-shaped mark on forehead (parent does not have this). More energetic expressions than parent. Slightly lighter cream color.

Parent and child look almost the same - clearly family. The only visible differences are size and the child's star mark. NOT identifiable as any specific animal. Extremely deformed, mascot-like, kawaii. Style: simple Japanese character design for comedy manga. Think Sumikko Gurashi level of abstraction.
```
**特徴**: 柴犬がベースだけど犬とわからないレベルに抽象化。親子はほぼ同じ見た目、サイズ差＋子の額の星マークだけが違い。

---

### B-2：ペンギンベース超デフォルメスタイル
```
Character design sheet for a 4-panel manga series. Clean simple style, thick outlines, flat pastel colors (soft blue, white, pale yellow), white background.

Two characters side by side with multiple expressions (cheerful, confused, dozing, excited):

1. Parent character: An extremely deformed round creature. Very loosely inspired by penguin but SO abstracted it is NOT recognizable as a penguin or any bird. A soft pear-shaped body, mostly pale blue with a white belly area. Two dot eyes, a tiny round yellow bump where a beak might be (but looks more like a button). No wings visible - just small round stubby arms. Stands on tiny feet. Calm gentle default expression. Medium size.

2. Child character: Almost identical shape and design as parent but 40% smaller. Same pear-shaped body, same dot eyes, same pale blue and white coloring. ONE difference only: wears a tiny round knit cap (pale yellow) on top of head (parent does not wear anything). Bouncier, more energetic expressions.

Parent and child are nearly identical - unmistakably family. Only differences: size and child's little hat. NOT identifiable as any real animal. Super deformed, round, squishy looking. Style: minimal Japanese mascot design for comedy manga.
```
**特徴**: ペンギンが元ネタだけど鳥に見えないレベルに丸く抽象化。親子の違いは大きさ＋子のニット帽だけ。

---

### B-3：うさぎベース超デフォルメスタイル
```
Character design sheet for a 4-panel manga series. Bold cute style, thick black outlines, flat bright pastel colors (soft pink, white, light mint), white background.

Two characters side by side with multiple expressions (grinning, shocked, napping, celebrating):

1. Parent character: An extremely deformed round creature. Very loosely inspired by rabbit but SO stylized it is NOT recognizable as a rabbit. A perfectly round body (like a mochi ball) with two long floppy shapes on top of head that droop down (could be ears, could be ribbons, could be anything). Dot eyes, tiny "w" shaped mouth. Soft pink and white coloring. No visible legs - appears to float or bounce. Small stubby arms. Relaxed sleepy default expression. Medium size.

2. Child character: Almost identical round mochi-ball shape as parent but 40% smaller. Same floppy head shapes, same dot eyes, same pink-white coloring. ONE difference only: child's floppy head shapes point UPWARD instead of drooping down (energetic vs relaxed). Livelier expressions, always slightly bouncing.

Parent and child are the same design - clearly a pair. Only differences: size and the direction of their head flaps (parent's droop down, child's stick up). NOT identifiable as any specific animal. Ultra-round, ultra-simple. Style: kawaii Japanese mascot for comedy manga. Sticker and stamp ready.
```
**特徴**: うさぎっぽいけど何かわからないもち型生物。親子の違いは大きさ＋頭のぴょこが垂れてる（親）か立ってる（子）かだけ。

## 使い方

1. 上のプロンプトをそのままNano Banana 2（Gemini）にコピペ
2. 生成された画像を見比べる
3. 気に入った方向性を選んで、そこからさらに調整

## 選ぶときのポイント

- **4コマ漫画で映えるか**（表情のバリエーションが出せるか）
- **小さいサムネイルでも認識できるか**（インスタのフィードで目立つか）
- **描き分けしやすいか**（毎回の投稿でキャラがブレないか）
- **グッズ展開を想像できるか**（将来的なIP価値）
